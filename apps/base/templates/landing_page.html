MoodyTunes

Welcome to MoodyTunes, the site to find music for your mood!

What is MoodyTunes?

Moodytunes is a site that helps you find songs to fit your desired mood. By browsing songs for a desired mood and voting on what songs make you feel that particular emotion, you not only build a playlist of songs for that emotion but also learn more about what kind of songs make you feel that emotion. We use this data to better suggest songs to you that make you feel that emotion, so you can tap into that particular mood. See the about page to learn more about how this process works.

How Do I Get Started?

To get started, simply create an account through our account creation page. Once your account has been created, you can immediately start browsing songs by desired emotion and building your playlists! If you happen to have a Spotify account, you can optionally login to Spotify through our site to listen to full songs on our site.
